about steve jobs

Steve Jobs was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and chief executive office(CEO) of Apple Inc.; CEO and majority shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios; a member of The Wall Disney Company's board of directors following its acquision of Pixar; and founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT Inc. Jobs is widely recognized as pioneer of microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with Apple's co-founder Steve Wozniak. Apples's revolutionary products, which includes the iPod, iPhone, iPad, are now seen as dictating the evolution of modern technology. Read more...
