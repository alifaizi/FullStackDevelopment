Patrick's Pizza

This is a awesome cheap pizza that you can't resist. Only 100 million dollars

Ingredients

  • dough
  • cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • flour
  • an oven

Steps

  1. roll the dough and put flour on it
  2. put tomato sauce on the dough
  3. put cheese on th tomato sauce
  4. add any toppings
  5. put in oven
  6. take out and enjoy
